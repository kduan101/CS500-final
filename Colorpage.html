Get to know your colors

Red

One of three primary colors, complimentary to green. It acts as a stimulant to increase your heart rate and energy as well as confidence.

Orange

Considered the color of creativity and joy. It stimulates appetite and sense of activity. Complementary to blue.

Yellow

Another primary color! Yellow is the brightest color we can see. Cheerful and energetic! Complementary to purple.

Green

The most relaxing color. It is associated nature and regeneration. Complementary to red.

Blue

Our final primary color. Blue represents wisdom and stability. It also has calming effects on the body. Complementary to orange.

Purple

The color of luxury and royalty. It is said to uplift the spirit while also calming one down. Complementary to yellow.

Black

Considered the absence of color. Black signifies mystery, power, elegance, and also evil.

White

Created by the mixing of all colors of light. White is associated with goodness and purity.
